The Man Behind The Media

James
Claybon

Producer · Content Creator · Interviewer

"Our goal is to elevate the entertainment industry with content that entertains from the inside out."

My name is James Claybon. King Solomon Entertainment Media has been in business for almost ten years. KSE has covered every event from the Oscars and Emmys to the Golden Globes, SAG Awards, and BET.

We've worked with most television streaming platforms and networks in business — from Bounce and BET to Viacom, Starz, Zeus, Amazon Prime, HBO, Showtime, Fox, Netflix, and ABC.
